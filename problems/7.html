Let \(\Omega \subset \mathbb{R}^3\) be a bounded box consisting of a physical domain and a Cartesian PML layer. For \(j = 1,2,3\), let

\[ \widetilde{x}_j(x_j) = x_j + \mathrm{i}\int_0^{x_j}\sigma_j(t)\,dt, \qquad \alpha_j := \frac{d\widetilde{x}_j}{dx_j} = 1 + \mathrm{i}\sigma_j, \]

where \(\sigma_j \ge 0\) vanishes in the physical domain and provides positive absorption in the PML layer. Define

\[ A = \operatorname{diag} \left( \frac{\alpha_2\alpha_3}{\alpha_1}, \frac{\alpha_1\alpha_3}{\alpha_2}, \frac{\alpha_1\alpha_2}{\alpha_3} \right). \]

The truncated Cartesian PML Maxwell equation is

\[ \operatorname{curl}\big(A\operatorname{curl}\mathbf{E}\big) - k^2 A^{-1}\mathbf{E} = A^{-1}\mathbf{f} \quad \text{in } \Omega, \]

with the outer boundary condition

\[ \mathbf{n}\times \mathbf{E} = 0 \quad \text{on } \partial\Omega. \]

The corresponding variational problem is: find \(\mathbf{E} \in H_0(\operatorname{curl};\Omega)\) such that

\[ a_{\mathrm{pml}}(\mathbf{E},\mathbf{v}) = \langle A^{-1}\mathbf{f},\mathbf{v}\rangle, \qquad \forall \mathbf{v} \in H_0(\operatorname{curl};\Omega), \]

where

\[ a_{\mathrm{pml}}(\mathbf{u},\mathbf{v}) := (A\operatorname{curl}\mathbf{u},\operatorname{curl}\mathbf{v})_{\Omega} - k^2(A^{-1}\mathbf{u},\mathbf{v})_{\Omega}, \]

and \((\cdot,\cdot)_\Omega\) denotes the complex \(L^2\)-inner product.

Assume that the PML coefficients are uniformly bounded and non-degenerate, and that the PML layer is sufficiently thick so that the PML reflection error is exponentially small.

Problem. Can one prove a \(k\)-explicit inf-sup estimate

\[ \inf_{0 \ne \mathbf{u} \in H_0(\operatorname{curl};\Omega)} \sup_{0 \ne \mathbf{v} \in H_0(\operatorname{curl};\Omega)} \frac{|a_{\mathrm{pml}}(\mathbf{u},\mathbf{v})|} {\|\mathbf{u}\|_{\operatorname{curl},k,\Omega}\|\mathbf{v}\|_{\operatorname{curl},k,\Omega}} \ge \beta_{\mathrm{pml}}(k) > 0, \]

where

\[ \|\mathbf{u}\|_{\operatorname{curl},k,\Omega}^2 := \|\operatorname{curl}\mathbf{u}\|_{L^2(\Omega)}^2 + k^2\|\mathbf{u}\|_{L^2(\Omega)}^2. \]

In particular, can one show that

\[ \beta_{\mathrm{pml}}(k) \ge C k^{-q} \]

for some \(q \ge 0\), with \(C\) independent of \(k\), under suitable PML thickness and absorption assumptions?
